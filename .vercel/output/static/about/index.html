About Me

Hi there, I'm Kishan. I build modern web products with a focus on efficiency and scalability, along with an interest in AI products. I currently work as a Full-Stack Developer at Quinence, where I lead and contribute to various projects.

Most recently, at Quinence, I have been leading the redevelopment of Fuschia Careers, a high-volume recruiting platform, enhancing its performance and functionality while integrating AI (LLM) to optimize recruiter workflows. Additionally, I work on a personalized tech training platform, Modern Fullstack, where I designed and developed the website architecture and backend, improving the platform’s performance by 10X. I was also involved in building the course content.

Previously, I worked with The Soulless Citadel, a blockchain NFT project, where I developed a high-performance, real-time game backend and smart contracts for the marketplace, facilitating over 2,200 transactions and generating sales of nearly $370,000.

I’ve always been passionate about web performance, optimizing systems to be fast and scalable, while maintaining attention to detail and a strong product mindset. Whether it's building email automation tools, crafting interactive courses, or working on AI-driven platforms, my goal is always to enhance the user experience through thoughtful engineering and innovation.

If you’re interested in collaborating or have a project in mind, feel free to reach out to me at hello@kishans.in.
